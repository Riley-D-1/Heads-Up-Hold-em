Rules for Heads Up Holdem

There are 4 diffrent hand types which are numbered below (Ordered from worst hand type to best)
  1. Split (3 of each)
  2. Quad (4 of the same type)
  3. Five Frenzy (5 of the same type)
  4. Flip Storm (6 of the same type)

Rules for Heads Up Holdem

  1. Player pays the buyin fee (Diffrent tables have diffrent buy in costs)
  2. Each player flips their 2 private coins
  3. Dealer is randomly selected
  4. he dealer then starts first betting round. In a clockwise order players can raise, match or fold. (Players must all match this and can raise it further to countinue playing or can fold)
  5. The dealer then flips 2 coins community coins.
  6. A second betting round occurs (starting on the dealer). In a clockwise order players can raise, match or fold. (Players must all match the bet or can fold)
  7. The dealer then flips the final 2 coins community coins.>
  8. Just count your heads and tails and use your highest number
  9. All personal coins are revealed and player with best hand type wins (If players tie on a hand, heads triumph over tails if there is still a tie then the pot is split equally between all)
